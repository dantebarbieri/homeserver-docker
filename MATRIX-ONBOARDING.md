# Joining the Book Club Chat

We're using **Matrix** for book club discussions — a free, private messaging app. Here's how to join in just a few minutes.

## Create Your Account

1. Open [**element.danteb.com**](https://element.danteb.com) in your browser
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Choose a **username** and **password**
4. If asked for a registration token, enter the one Dante shared with you

Done — you have an account! ✓

## Join the Book Club

Once you're logged in, click this link to jump into our room:

👉 [**Join Book Club**](https://element.danteb.com/#/room/#bookclub:danteb.com)

## Chat on Your Phone (Optional)

Download the **Element** app: [**element.io/download**](https://element.io/download) (iPhone & Android)

To sign in on the app:
1. Tap **"Sign In"**
2. Change the homeserver to `matrix.danteb.com`
3. Enter your username and password

## Already Have a Matrix Account?

Matrix is federated — accounts on different servers can talk to each other, just like email. Join from your existing app:

👉 [**matrix.to/#/#bookclub:danteb.com**](https://matrix.to/#/#bookclub:danteb.com)
